Welcome to the Smart Calculator App!

To utilize the Smart Calculator, follow these simple steps:
  1. Basic Arithmetic Operations: Use the large, easy-to-read buttons to perform addition (+), subtraction (-), multiplication (*), and division (/) operations. For instance, to add 5 and 3, tap on the "5" button, followed by the "+" button, and then the "3" button. Finally, hit the "=" button to view the result.
  2. Clearing the Display: If you need to clear the display, press the "AC" button. This will reset the calculator to zero, ready for your next calculation.
  3. Percentage Calculations: Need to calculate a percentage? It's a breeze with our calculator. Simply input the number, followed by the "%" button to get the percentage value.
  4. Decimal Numbers: For decimal numbers, use the "." button to insert the decimal point.
  5. Negation: To toggle between positive and negative numbers, tap the "+/-" button.
  6. Equal Sign: After inputting your expression, press the "=" button to see the result displayed clearly on the calculator's screen.
  7. Stay Organized: Keep track of your calculations effortlessly with the large display, ensuring accuracy and efficiency in your computations.
